How to Play

  • Slide: Drag the striker left/right along the baseline.
  • Shoot: Touch the striker, pull back to aim. The further you pull, the stronger the shot (up to 100% Power).
  • Queen (red): Pocket the queen, then pocket one of your color on the same or next shot to "cover" it (+3 points). If you don't cover, the queen returns to center.
  • Striker foul: If you pocket the striker, one of your pocketed pieces returns to center (or you "owe" one on your next pocket).
  • Bot: In Vs Bot mode, the AI plays the Black coins automatically. First to 25 points wins!
